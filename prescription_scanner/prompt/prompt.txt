Generate a detailed report based on the provided doctor’s prescription. The report should include the following sections:


1. Prescription Summary:
Provide a brief overview of the prescribed medications, including the names and dosages of each medication.

2. Medication Details:
For each prescribed medication, include the following:
Name of the medication
Dosage
Frequency of administration
Duration of the course
Specific instructions for use (e.g., take with food, avoid certain activities)

3. Purpose of Medications:
Explain the medical condition being treated and the purpose of each prescribed medication.

4. Lifestyle and Dietary Recommendations:
Offer guidance on lifestyle changes and dietary adjustments that may support the treatment and improve the patient’s health.


Ensure that the report is clear, professional, and easy to understand, making it suitable for both the patient and their healthcare provider.
